# Sample 4 — Decline or Re-Scope a Low Budget

**Subject:** Scope options for `[Campaign]`

Hi `[Name]`,

Thank you for sharing the `[currency and amount]` budget. I cannot take on the current combination of `[deliverables]`, `[usage rights]`, and `[exclusivity or timeline]` at that amount.

If useful, I can offer one of these paths:

1. a smaller scope at the stated budget: `[one deliverable]`, `[one revision]`, and `[limited organic use with a defined term]`; or
2. the original scope for `[your supported quote]`, subject to final written terms.

If neither option fits, I will decline this campaign, but I appreciate the clear budget information.

Best,

`[Your name]`

Remove any option you are not genuinely willing to deliver. Set your own quote from the real workload, rights, risk, and opportunity cost; this sample does not prescribe a market rate.
